About PEKAD Global Resources

PEKAD GLOBAL RESOURCES NIG LTD is a premier Nigerian agro-processing company dedicated to producing and exporting premium red palm oil and natural agricultural powders. From our headquarters in Lagos, we bridge the gap between traditional African farming and the modern global market.

Our Mission is to process and export high-quality products that meet strict international quality and safety standards. We are committed to promoting healthy living by offering products that are free from artificial additives, showcasing the richness of African agricultural produce to consumers worldwide.

We specialize in a diverse range of natural products, including our signature Premium Red Palm Oil, as well as nutrient-dense powders like Zobo (Hibiscus), Tiger Nut, Turmeric, Dates, and Ginger.

Our target market spans across international importers, wholesalers, retailers, and health-conscious consumers who value authenticity and nutritional purity. Whether you are a food manufacturer or a retail shopper, PEKAD ensures excellence in every pack.

We take immense pride in our sustainable sourcing and modern processing techniques. By working closely with local farmers and utilizing advanced drying and milling technology, we ensure that every product retains its natural nutrients, vibrant color, and authentic flavor.
